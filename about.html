About Me

A developer with purpose

Hello! I'm Teresiah Musyoka, a junior web developer based in Kenya with a genuine love for building digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new design trends, sharpening my skills, and looking for ways to grow as a developer. I'm always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and connections.
